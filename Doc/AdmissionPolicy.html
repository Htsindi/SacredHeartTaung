LEARNER ADMISSION POLICY 2019

 

We aim to mould the character of our children through spiritual guidance and quality education so that they may become useful citizens who will serve the society and the country selflessly.

We provide an educational environment conducive to the holistic development of the learner; this includes the formation of attitudes and values inspired by the spirituality of unity in the Catholic Church.

We admit South African boys and girls who are willing to uphold the morality and values based on Christian principles; who have the ability to meet our internal standards of education; whose families are able to commit themselves to our school fees; who have the aptitude to develop and realise their potential as participating, responsible and compassionate citizens of our country.

We are a fully-fledged Catholic School with Religious Education. We also aim to uplift and empower learners who may be from a disadvantaged background and who have the potential to benefit from our kind of school and who will carry the ethos into their community.

Where vacancies exist we accept boys and girls who are not South African citizens.

 

ENROLMENT PROCEDURE

 

Requirement for intake:

1.      Learners need to be of the appropriate age group for the grade to ensure that our holistic developmental stages are in place as these impacts future achievements.

    To clarify;

·        Grade R learners will be 5 turning 6

·        Grade 1 learners will be 6 turning 7

·        Grade 2 learners will be 7 turning 8

·        Grade 3 learners will be 8 turning 9 etc

2.      Learners need to have completed the previous stage at a recognised school.

3.      There must be a willingness to participate in Religious Education classes and our school sacred masses and services.

4.      Sacred Heart school reserves the right to contact the learner’s previous school to confirm the learner’s performance, if the learner has left the school in good standing or any other pertinent information at the school.                                                                                                                   1

5.      There needs to be a place available. Places are secured on full payment and allocated in that order in the order of enrolment until the classes are full.                                                             

6.      Parents/Guardians need to be able to cope with the fee payments and the extras required in order to participate fully.

7.      Parents/Guardians of learners must agree to abide by Sacred Heart School rules, regulations and ethos.

8.      An agreement must be made by parents and learners that they will abide by the school policies.

9.      Learners may be required to do an entrance interview or test.

10. The child’s needs should be such that our facilities are suitable for their holistic development.

11. Children of our staff members are given priority in the enrolment process.

12. In case a learner is leaving the school, a clearance form should be filled before a transfer letter is issued.

 

Inclusion policy

 

Our present facilities enable us to include children with mild to moderate educational problems, limited emotional difficulties and slight physical disabilities. Where we are confident that we are able to assist these children through remediation and support systems, to develop suitable enduring coping mechanisms which will allow them to be the best that they can be, we can in all conscience admit them.

 

In cases where prospective learners require greater assistance than our facilities can offer, and where more than two learning areas require significant therapy or where a facilitator is required for the child to function effectively it is our obligation to recommend a suitable alternative. Therefore, offering informed and experienced guidance for solutions to suit the individual child’s specific needs.

·        Facilities

5.      Parents/Guardians fill in the application form and pay the required fee in full i.e. registration fee, uniform fee, stationery fee and the first month of schooling fees. Attached a colour ID/passport photo of the learner on the admission form. This form is then placed in the admission file.

6.      Parents are requested to adhere to the requirements of the application form.
